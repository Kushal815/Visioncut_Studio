Frequently Asked Questions

How do you optimize videos for organic growth?

+

We use a data-driven approach: strategic hooks in the first 3 seconds to stop scrolling, retention-focused pacing to maximize watch time, pattern interrupts every 7-10 seconds to maintain attention, and platform-specific formatting (vertical for TikTok/Reels, horizontal for YouTube). We also optimize CTAs based on platform algorithms and viewer behavior patterns.

What's your typical turnaround time?

+

Standard turnaround is 24-48 hours for most projects. Rush delivery (12-24 hours) is available for urgent needs. Longer projects like wedding films or documentaries typically take 5-7 days. We understand that consistent posting is crucial for algorithm performance, so we prioritize fast delivery without compromising quality.

Do you provide multiple format versions?

+

Absolutely! Every project includes horizontal (16:9 for YouTube), vertical (9:16 for TikTok/Reels/Shorts), and square (1:1 for Instagram feed) versions. We also create platform-specific edits with optimized CTAs, captions, and thumbnail frames. This maximizes your content ROI across all platforms.

Can you help with video strategy and planning?

+

Yes! We offer strategic consultation to help you plan content that aligns with your business goals. This includes hook scripting, content repurposing strategies, posting schedules, and performance analysis. We'll help you understand what's working and how to double down on winning formats.

What if I need revisions?

+

All projects include 2 rounds of revisions. We want you to be completely satisfied with the final product. Most clients are happy with the first version, but we're committed to refining until it's perfect. Additional revisions beyond the included rounds are available at a minimal fee.

How much does video editing cost?

Social media clips start at $150, product videos from $300, and comprehensive packages (multiple formats + repurposing) from $500. Contact us for a custom quote based on your specific needs.
